## Source Content
Social Media channels are intended for 13+ users only. Marketing communications and dialogue, which are linked to LEGO products or the LEGO brand must not be seen to be targeting children. Equally, engaging in dialogue through comments and posts with a person who is aged 12 and under is not permitted under any circumstances. See ill. 62.
