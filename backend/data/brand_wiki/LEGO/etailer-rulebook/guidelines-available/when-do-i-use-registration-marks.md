## Source Content
The minimum requirement for websites is to apply a registration mark ® to the LEGO and DUPLO word marks the first time they appear on the landing page header, the product header or banner ads. See ill. 51.

On the next page you will find a selection of Do's and Don'ts when working with the LEGO word mark and trademarks.
