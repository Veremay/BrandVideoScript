## Source Content
The LEGO Group provides assets to create e.g. the product, theme or brand pages, these are to be the first choice of use. The assets can be found on the LEGO Content Hub. Only when the material is not available, can other assets be used, in agreement with the account manager. Equally, the LEGO Social Media team will provide assets, if needed, for any social media activity. Never use images or videos downloaded from other sources or the internet.
