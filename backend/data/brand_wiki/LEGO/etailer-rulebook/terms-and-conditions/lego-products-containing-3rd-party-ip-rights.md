## Source Content
Special rules apply for asset usage concerning LEGO Products incorporating 3<sup>rd</sup> party IP rights (e.g., LEGO Star Wars™ products or LEGO Disney products). The Customer should find the best solution in cooperation with the account manager.

The above-mentioned products also have their own legal lines which can be supplied by the account manager.
