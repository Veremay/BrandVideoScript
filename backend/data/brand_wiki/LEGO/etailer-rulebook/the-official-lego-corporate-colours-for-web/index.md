## Source Content
The LEGO Group has chosen a colour range for corporate use. The colours should be used in situations where you create your own graphic elements such as the LEGO® Brick for decorative use. Other LEGO colours including brick colours can be found in the LEGO Colour Guide. This guide and other guidelines are available on the LEGO Content Hub.
